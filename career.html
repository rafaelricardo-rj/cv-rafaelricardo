Time for change

After 8 years working with satellite and pay TV telecommunications, I decided to invest in software development which was always the one I liked to study. I started studying Computer Engineering in 2014 and got a job at an Internet Service Provider(ISP) where I could gain experience with MySQL database and web applications using PHP Frameworks (Codeigniter, Laravel), Javascript and Linux systems as well as virtualization on VMWare ESXi.

Working for 4 years in this small companie, when the oil crisis in Brazil happend, I decided to continue studying abroad seeking first to improve English as a second language and later to do a Higher Diploma in Science of Computing where I could consolidate my knowledge and being able to contribute to other teams in new market opportunities.

Currently studying at a university in Dublin, Ireland, I am close to completing this level 8 postgraduate program and available to work in internship or junior level vacancies.
